Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite friends to WE DUMP?

Currently, friends need to sign up with their email. Share the app link with them and they can create their own account. In future updates, we'll add invitation codes for better privacy control.

Is WE DUMP really free?

Yes! WE DUMP is completely free to use. We believe in keeping private social spaces accessible. We may add optional premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How is my data protected?

We use Firebase for secure authentication and storage. All photos are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't sell your data or use it for advertising. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your photos and data from our servers.

What are the file size limits?

Each photo can be up to 5MB. We support JPG, PNG, and GIF formats. For best performance, we recommend uploading photos that are 2000px or less on the longest side.
