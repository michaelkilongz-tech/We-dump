WE DUMP

Private photo wall for close friends

Our Story

WE DUMP was born from a simple idea: creating a private, stress-free space where close friends can share their everyday moments without the pressure of social media perfection.

In a world where social media often feels performative, we wanted to build something authentic—a digital wall where you can just "dump" your photos and enjoy the collective memories with your closest circle.

Why WE DUMP?

Private & Secure

Only invited friends can see your photos. No public sharing.

No Pressure

Share unedited, authentic moments. No likes, no followers.

Close Friends Only

Designed for your inner circle. Quality over quantity.

Always Growing

The wall keeps expanding with new memories from your crew.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: Your photos stay within your trusted circle
  • Authenticity: Be real, be yourself, no filters needed
  • Simplicity: Easy to use, no complicated features
  • Community: Building stronger bonds through shared memories
  • Respect: Everyone's privacy and boundaries matter
